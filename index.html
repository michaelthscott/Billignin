September 2005

somehow i'd got interested in Gertrude Stein

i wanted to know what i thought - how it worked - why it happened - the whole Gertrude Stein thing

key to this was to see where she walked the dog

it would've been better to go in the summer - i'd go earlier if i was you

start from Paris - TGV out of the Gare de Lyon - straight and fast - except for the crawl up to Chambéry - we stayed in Aix-les-Bains

first day - we walked down to Lac de Bourget

second day - we took the train from Aix-les-Bains to Culoz

third day - we took the train from Aix-les-Bains to Chambréy - took the bus to Belley - walked from Belley to Billignin - before we came back we had a look at what once was the Pernollet Hotel

if i were you i'd swap days two and three
