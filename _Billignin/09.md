there is one last thing to do before the bus comes - we have to see what is left of the Pernollet Hotel - where Gertrude and Alice stayed before they moved into the house at Billignin

as I said - the Pernollet is now an old peoples' home - and has been somewhat rebuilt - but you can go through the passageway and see what must have been the garage of the hotel - here ... lost generation ...

that's it - Belley is done - depending on how much time you have before the bus arrives you can wander down past the tourist office again and see the home of Brillat Savarin
